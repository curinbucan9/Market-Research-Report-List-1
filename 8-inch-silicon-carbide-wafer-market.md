<p><p>The 8-inch silicon carbide (SiC) wafer market is primarily divided into two types: the 8-inch polished wafer and the 8-inch epitaxial wafer. Polished wafers undergo extensive surface finishing to achieve a high level of flatness and surface quality, ideal for various semiconductor applications. Epitaxial wafers, on the other hand, have a thin layer of silicon carbide deposited on their surface, enhancing electrical properties and enabling high-efficiency devices. Both types are essential for power electronics and advanced technology applications.</p></p>
