<p><p>The ablation equipment market comprises various technologies, notably ultrasound ablation and radiofrequency ablation. Ultrasound ablation employs high-frequency sound waves to destroy targeted tissue, often used in soft tissue surgeries. Radiofrequency ablation utilizes electromagnetic waves to generate heat, effectively eliminating abnormal tissues, commonly in cardiac and tumor treatments. Both methods offer minimally invasive alternatives to traditional surgery, enhancing recovery times and reducing complications, making them increasingly popular in modern medical practices for treating various conditions.</p></p>
