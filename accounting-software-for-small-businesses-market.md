<p><p>Accounting software for small businesses comes in two primary market types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted online, allowing for easy access from any device with internet connectivity. This model typically offers subscription pricing and automatic updates. In contrast, on-premises software is installed locally on a company’s hardware, providing full control but requiring upfront costs and ongoing maintenance. Both options cater to different needs, with cloud-based solutions emphasizing flexibility and scalability, while on-premises software focuses on security and customization.</p></p>
