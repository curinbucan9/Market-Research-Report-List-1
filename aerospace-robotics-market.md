<p><p>The aerospace robotics market includes various types of robots based on their design and functionality. SCARA robots feature a rigid arm with a spherical working envelope, ideal for assembly tasks. Articulated robots, resembling a human arm, are versatile and suitable for complex movements in manufacturing. Cartesian robots have linear motion along three axes, perfect for pick-and-place operations. Other market segments might include collaborative robots (cobots) and Delta robots, which enhance automation and efficiency in aerospace applications, driving innovation and productivity.</p></p>
