<p><p>The Automotive Controller Area Network Flexible Data-Rate (CAN FD) transceiver market is segmented into isolated and non-isolated types. Isolated transceivers provide electrical isolation between different network segments to protect against voltage spikes and noise, enhancing reliability in critical automotive applications. Non-isolated transceivers, on the other hand, allow direct connections between nodes without isolation, typically offering cost advantages and simpler designs. Each type serves distinct requirements, balancing between safety, performance, and economic efficiency in automotive networks.</p></p>
