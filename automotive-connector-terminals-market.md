<p><strong>The Automotive Connector Terminals Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Pre-insulated Forms</li><li>Non-insulated Forms</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive connector terminals market can be categorized into two main types: pre-insulated forms and non-insulated forms. Pre-insulated terminals come with a layer of insulation, providing enhanced electrical safety and reducing the risk of short circuits. They are often used in environments where moisture or corrosion is a concern. In contrast, non-insulated terminals are simpler and may offer better conductivity but require additional protective measures to prevent contact with conductive surfaces. Both types play crucial roles in vehicle wiring systems.</p></p>
