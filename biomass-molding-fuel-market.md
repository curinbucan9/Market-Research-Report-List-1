<p><p>The biomass molding fuel market comprises two primary types: granular and block. Granular biomass fuels are small, pellet-like particles that offer high energy efficiency and easier handling, making them suitable for various combustion systems. In contrast, block biomass fuels are denser and larger, often in the form of briquettes, providing a longer burn time and higher heat output. Both types serve as renewable energy sources, appealing to industries aiming to reduce carbon footprints and enhance sustainability in energy consumption.</p></p>
