<p><p>The bistable solenoid valve market can be categorized into two main types: detent type and latching type. Detent type solenoid valves maintain their position through a mechanical latch, ensuring that the valve remains in its last state even after the electric signal is removed. In contrast, latching type solenoid valves utilize magnetic latching to achieve a similar effect but are designed to toggle states with minimal power, enhancing energy efficiency. Both types find applications in automation, fluid control, and robotics.</p></p>
