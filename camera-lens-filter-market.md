<p><p>The **Camera Lens Filter Market** includes various types designed to control light wavelengths. **Band Pass Filters** allow a specific range of wavelengths to pass, blocking others, ideal for precise color or spectral imaging. **Long Pass Filters** transmit longer wavelengths while blocking shorter ones, useful for infrared photography. **Short Pass Filters** do the opposite, permitting shorter wavelengths and blocking longer ones, often used in UV imaging. **Other filters** include neutral density, polarizing, or specialty filters for unique photographic or industrial applications.</p></p>
