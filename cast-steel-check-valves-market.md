<p><strong>The Cast Steel Check Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Raised-Flange End</li><li>Ring-Type Joint End</li><li>Butt-Weld End</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cast steel check valves are essential in controlling flow direction in various piping systems. The market features three main types based on end connections. Raised-flange end check valves have a protruding rim for easy bolting, ensuring a secure fit. Ring-type joint end valves utilize a circular groove for a reliable seal, ideal for high-pressure applications. Butt-weld end valves are directly welded into piping, providing a streamlined design and minimizing potential leak points. Each type serves specific operational needs across industries.</p></p>
