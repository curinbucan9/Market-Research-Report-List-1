<p><p>The dairy starter culture market is segmented into three main types: mesophilic, thermophilic, and probiotics. Mesophilic cultures thrive at moderate temperatures, commonly used in cheese and yogurt production, enhancing flavor and texture. Thermophilic cultures operate at higher temperatures, ideal for making yogurt and certain cheeses, contributing to a firmer texture. Probiotic cultures are specifically designed to add health benefits, promoting gut health and wellness. Each type plays a crucial role in the dairy industry, influencing product quality and consumer health.</p></p>
