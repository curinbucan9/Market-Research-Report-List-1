<p><p>Detection switches are critical components in various applications, categorized mainly into SPDT (Single Pole Double Throw) and SPST-NC (Single Pole Single Throw Normally Closed) types. SPDT switches can connect to one of two circuits, allowing versatile control and sensing functions, while SPST-NC switches remain closed by default, opening only when triggered. These switches are widely used in safety systems, automation, and control panels, contributing to efficient monitoring and triggering mechanisms in industrial and consumer applications.</p></p>
