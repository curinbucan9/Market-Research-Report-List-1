<p><p>The detergents market is classified into four main types: Anionic detergents, which carry a negative charge and are effective in removing dirt and stains; Cationic detergents, which have a positive charge and are often used as fabric softeners and disinfectants; Non-ionic detergents, which are neutral and suitable for delicate fabrics and sensitive skin; and Zwitterionic (Amphoteric) detergents, which can carry both positive and negative charges, making them versatile in various applications. Each type serves specific cleaning needs in household and industrial contexts.</p></p>
