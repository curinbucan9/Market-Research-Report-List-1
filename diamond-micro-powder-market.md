<p><p>The Diamond Micro Powder market comprises two main types: Natural Diamond Powder and Synthetic Diamond Powder. Natural Diamond Powder is derived from mined diamonds, offering high purity and hardness, making it ideal for precision polishing and cutting applications. Synthetic Diamond Powder, produced through high-pressure, high-temperature (HPHT) or chemical vapor deposition (CVD) methods, provides consistent quality and cost-effectiveness, widely used in industrial abrasives, electronics, and coatings. Both types cater to diverse industries, with natural powder being premium and synthetic powder being scalable and versatile.</p></p>
