<p><p>Distributed Photovoltaic Inverter markets are categorized into three types: </p><p>1. **Grid-tied**: These inverters connect directly to the electrical grid, allowing users to sell excess power back to the grid, enhancing energy efficiency and reducing costs.</p><p>2. **Grid/Hybrid**: This system combines grid connectivity with energy storage, enabling users to switch between grid power and stored energy based on availability and demand.</p><p>3. **Off-grid**: These inverters operate independently from the grid, relying on battery storage to provide power in remote locations, ensuring energy access without grid dependency.</p></p>
