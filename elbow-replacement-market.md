<p><ul><li>Partial Elbow Replacement</li><li>Total Elbow Replacement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The elbow replacement market comprises two main types: partial elbow replacement and total elbow replacement. Partial elbow replacement involves replacing only the damaged portions of the elbow joint, preserving as much of the natural bone and soft tissue as possible, making it suitable for less severe arthritis or trauma cases. In contrast, total elbow replacement entails replacing the entire joint with prosthetic components, ideal for advanced arthritis or significant joint damage, providing enhanced pain relief and improved mobility.</p></p>
