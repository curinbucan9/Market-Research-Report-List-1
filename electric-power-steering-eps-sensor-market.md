<p><strong>The Electric Power Steering (EPS) Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Torque Only Sensor(TOS)</li><li>Torque Angle Sensor(TAS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Electric Power Steering (EPS) sensor market is primarily categorized into Torque Only Sensors (TOS) and Torque Angle Sensors (TAS). TOS measures the amount of torque applied to the steering wheel, providing essential feedback for steering assistance without considering the angle. In contrast, TAS assesses both the torque and the angle of the steering wheel, enabling more precise control and responsiveness. These sensors enhance driving safety and comfort, driving the demand for EPS systems in modern vehicles.</p></p>
