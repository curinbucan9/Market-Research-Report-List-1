<p><p>The flanged outlet adaptor market consists of three primary types: straight, elbow, and side opening tubes. Straight adaptors provide a direct connection for streamlined flow, ideal for minimal resistance in pipelines. Elbow adaptors are designed for changing flow direction, making them essential in systems with space constraints. Side opening tubes enable lateral access, facilitating maintenance and attachment of additional equipment. Together, these types cater to various industrial applications, enhancing efficiency in fluid transport and system versatility.</p></p>
