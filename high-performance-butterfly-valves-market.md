<p><strong>The High Performance Butterfly Valves Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Wafer Type</li><li>Lug Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The high-performance butterfly valves market features two main types: wafer and lug. Wafer-type valves are designed to fit between flanges, providing a lightweight, compact solution ideal for space-constrained applications. They offer easy installation and are typically cost-effective. Conversely, lug-type valves feature threaded inserts on the valve body, allowing installation without needing downstream piping support. This design enhances maintenance accessibility and is suitable for bi-directional flow applications, making both types essential for varied industrial needs in fluid control systems.</p></p>
