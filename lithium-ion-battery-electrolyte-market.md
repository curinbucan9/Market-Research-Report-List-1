<p><p>The lithium-ion battery electrolyte market comprises two primary types: liquid and solid electrolytes. On the other hand, solid electrolytes, consisting of solid lithium-ion conductive materials, offer enhanced safety and stability, reducing risks of leakage and thermal runaway.
