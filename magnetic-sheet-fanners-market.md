<p><p>The Magnetic Sheet Fanners market is segmented into two primary types: Permanent Magnet fanners and Electromagnetic fanners. Permanent Magnet fanners utilize fixed magnets to generate a magnetic field that effectively separates and aligns sheets of metal or other materials. In contrast, Electromagnetic fanners use variable electromagnetic fields, allowing for adjustable force and more control over the separation process. Each type offers distinct advantages, making them suitable for different industrial applications based on the specific handling and processing needs.</p></p>
