<p><p>Maleic Anhydride Grafted Polyethylene (MAPE) is categorized into two primary market types based on density: High Density Polyethylene (HDPE) and Low Density Polyethylene (LDPE). HDPE-MAPE typically offers superior mechanical strength and thermal resistance, making it suitable for applications like rigid packaging and automotive parts. In contrast, LDPE-MAPE features enhanced flexibility and lower density, making it ideal for applications in flexible packaging and films. Both types leverage maleic anhydride grafting to improve adhesion and compatibility with polar materials.</p></p>
