<p><p>The medical and biological sensors market encompasses various types of sensors that monitor and measure health-related parameters. Temperature sensors track body temperature, crucial for diagnosing fevers or monitoring patient conditions. Pressure sensors measure blood pressure or intravascular pressure, essential in critical care. Photo optic sensors detect light wavelengths for applications like pulse oximetry. Liquid level sensors gauge fluid levels in medical containers or IV bags, ensuring proper medication delivery. Other sensors include gas, humidity, and motion sensors, expanding healthcare monitoring capabilities.</p></p>
