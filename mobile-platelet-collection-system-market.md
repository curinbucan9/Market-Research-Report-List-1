<p><p>The Mobile Platelet Collection System market features two primary types: centrifugation and membrane filtration. Centrifugation utilizes centrifugal force to separate platelets from blood components, allowing for efficient collection and concentration. Membrane filtration, on the other hand, employs selective membranes to separate platelets based on size and concentration, ensuring high purity. Both methods prioritize patient safety and comfort while enabling rapid processing and collection of platelets, catering to the increasing demand for blood transfusions and therapeutic applications.</p></p>
