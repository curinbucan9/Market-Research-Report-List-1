Lenalidomide is an immunomodulatory drug effective for certain genetic subtypes of MDS, enhancing immune response and suppressing tumor growth. Deferasirox is a chelating agent used to manage iron overload in MDS patients undergoing frequent blood transfusions. Together, these treatments target various aspects of MDS to improve patient outcomes.</p></p>
