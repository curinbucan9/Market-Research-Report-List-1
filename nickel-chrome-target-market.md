<p><p>Nickel chrome targets are essential in various applications, primarily in the semiconductor industry and thin-film deposition processes. The two main market types include plane targets and rotating targets. Plane targets provide a consistent deposition surface for uniform coating on flat substrates, making them ideal for applications requiring precision. Rotating targets, on the other hand, enhance material utilization and uniformity by continuously rotating during deposition, allowing for larger area coverage and improved efficiency, thus catering to different manufacturing needs.</p></p>
