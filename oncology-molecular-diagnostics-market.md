<p><p>The oncology molecular diagnostics market includes various technologies for cancer detection and characterization. PCR (Polymerase Chain Reaction) amplifies specific DNA sequences for analysis. INDAAT (Isothermal Nucleic Acid Amplification Tests) enables rapid detection without thermal cycling. DNA sequencing identifies genetic mutations, while NGS (Next-Generation Sequencing) offers comprehensive genomic insights. In situ hybridization visualizes specific nucleic acid sequences within tissues. DNA microarrays analyze gene expression across multiple genes simultaneously. Other market types may include image-based assays and proteomics, enhancing cancer diagnostics.</p></p>
