<p><p>The photoswitch market encompasses various types that cater to different applications in liquid level control. Single Point Float Photoswitches detect a specific liquid level using one float, ideal for simple on/off applications. Double Float Photoswitches employ two floats to provide both high and low-level detection, enhancing control for varying liquid volumes. Three Float Photoswitches offer even more precision, monitoring three distinct levels, making them suitable for complex systems that require multiple level triggers. Each type serves unique operational needs in industrial and commercial settings.</p></p>
