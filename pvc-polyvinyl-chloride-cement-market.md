<p><p>The PVC Cement market primarily caters to two types of pipes: Schedule 40 and Schedule 80 PVC pipes. Schedule 40 PVC pipes, which are lighter and used for lower pressure applications, typically find usage in residential plumbing and irrigation systems. In contrast, Schedule 80 PVC pipes have thicker walls, making them suitable for higher pressure applications, such as industrial settings and chemical processing. Both segments contribute to the overall growth of the PVC Cement market, driven by construction and infrastructure demands.</p></p>
