<p><p>The radial flow centrifugal pump market consists of three main types: single-stage, two-stage, and multi-stage pumps. Single-stage pumps have one impeller and are suitable for low-pressure applications. Two-stage pumps feature two impellers, enabling them to generate higher pressures for medium-scale operations. Multi-stage pumps contain multiple impellers, making them ideal for high-pressure and high-flow applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, ranging from water supply to chemical processing, by efficiently moving fluids under varying conditions.</p></p>
