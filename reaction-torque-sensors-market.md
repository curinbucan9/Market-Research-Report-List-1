The market includes various types such as Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) sensors, which utilize acoustic waves to detect torque changes; optical sensors that use light properties for precise measurements; strain gauges, which measure the deformation of materials under applied torque; and magnetoelastic sensors, which rely on magnetic properties of materials to gauge torque. Each type offers distinct advantages for various industrial applications.</p></p>
