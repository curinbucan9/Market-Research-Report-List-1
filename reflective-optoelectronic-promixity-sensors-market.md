<p><p>The Reflective Optoelectronic Proximity Sensors market comprises mainly two types: IR Reflective and LED Reflective sensors. IR Reflective Proximity Sensors utilize infrared light to detect nearby objects by measuring the reflection off the surface. They are commonly used in automation and safety applications. On the other hand, LED Reflective Proximity Sensors employ visible light-emitting diodes to achieve similar detection capabilities, offering advantages such as higher visibility and energy efficiency. Both types find applications in industrial automation, robotics, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
