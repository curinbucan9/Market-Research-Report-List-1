<p><strong>The Reinforcement Steel Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Deformed Steel</li><li>Mild Steel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reinforcement steel market comprises primarily two types: deformed steel and mild steel. Deformed steel features ridges or patterns that enhance bonding with concrete, making it ideal for high-strength applications in construction. Meanwhile, mild steel, characterized by its ductility and malleability, is often used in non-load-bearing structures and general construction. Both types cater to diverse building needs, with deformed steel typically preferred for structural reinforcement, while mild steel finds its place in less demanding applications.</p></p>
