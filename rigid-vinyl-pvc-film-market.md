<p><p>The rigid vinyl (PVC) film market is divided primarily into two types: clear rigid vinyl and opaque rigid vinyl. Clear rigid vinyl is transparent, allowing for visibility of products and is commonly used in packaging applications, displays, and medical products. Opaque rigid vinyl, on the other hand, is non-transparent, offering privacy and protection for contents; it's often used in signage, graphic applications, and packaging that requires branding and aesthetics. Both types serve diverse industries with unique functionality.</p></p>
