<p><p>The robotic gripper system market comprises two primary types: electric and pneumatic grippers. Electric grippers utilize motors and sensors to provide precise control and responsiveness, making them ideal for applications requiring accuracy and adaptability. They are typically quieter and require less maintenance. In contrast, pneumatic grippers operate using compressed air, offering high gripping force and speed, suited for high-speed assembly lines. Each type caters to different needs, influencing their application across various industries including manufacturing, logistics, and automation.</p></p>
