<p><ul><li>Mercury Xenon Light Sourced</li><li>Metal Halide Sourced</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Round portlights for ships come in two main market types based on their light sources: Mercury Xenon and Metal Halide. </p><p>Mercury Xenon light-sourced portlights utilize a combination of mercury vapor and xenon gas to produce bright, efficient illumination, ideal for depth visibility and energy efficiency. </p><p>In contrast, Metal Halide lights generate illumination through an electric arc between tungsten electrodes, providing a high lumen output and excellent color rendering, making them suitable for various maritime applications. Both types enhance safety and visibility on vessels.</p></p>
