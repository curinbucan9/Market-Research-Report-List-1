<p><p>Sanitary plug valves are crucial components in hygienic piping systems, primarily used in the food, beverage, and pharmaceutical industries. The two main types are sanitary 2-way and 3-way plug valves. </p><p>Sanitary 2-way plug valves enable flow control in a single direction, making them suitable for on/off applications. Sanitary 3-way plug valves allow for flow diversion or mixing of fluids, providing versatility in complex systems. Both types are designed for easy cleaning and maintenance, ensuring compliance with sanitary standards.</p></p>
