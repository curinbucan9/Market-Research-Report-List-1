<p><p>The single-pass digital textile printing machine market comprises primarily two types: inkjet digital printing machines and dye-sublimation digital printing machines. Inkjet digital printing machines utilize inkjet technology to apply ink directly onto fabric, allowing for high-resolution designs and versatility across various textiles. In contrast, dye-sublimation digital printing machines involve a process where heat transfers dye from a solid state to a gas, embedding vibrant colors into polyester textiles. Both types cater to the growing demand for customized and efficient textile production.</p></p>
