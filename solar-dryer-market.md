<p><ul><li>Direct Type</li><li>Indirect Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The solar dryer market is primarily segmented into two types: direct and indirect solar dryers. Direct solar dryers utilize sunlight directly to heat and dry the products, often employing transparent materials to trap heat. In contrast, indirect solar dryers collect solar energy and transfer it to the drying chamber, protecting the products from direct sunlight while allowing for controlled drying temperatures. Both types cater to various applications, such as agricultural produce and food preservation, supporting sustainable drying methods.</p></p>
