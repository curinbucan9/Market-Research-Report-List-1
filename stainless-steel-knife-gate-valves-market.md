<p><p>The stainless steel knife gate valves market features two main types: metal seated and resilient seated knife gate valves. Metal seated knife gate valves are designed for high-pressure and high-temperature applications, offering durability and longevity, making them ideal for abrasive materials. Resilient seated knife gate valves, on the other hand, utilize a soft seat design that provides a tighter seal and is suitable for lower pressure applications with less abrasive substances, ensuring effective flow control and reduced leakage.</p></p>
