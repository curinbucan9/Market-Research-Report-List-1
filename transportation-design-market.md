<p><p>Transportation design encompasses two main market types: external design and interior design. External design focuses on the outer aesthetics and functionality of vehicles, including shape, surface materials, and aerodynamics, catering to branding and consumer appeal. Interior design, on the other hand, emphasizes the user experience within the vehicle, addressing ergonomics, layout, materials, and technology integration to enhance comfort and usability. Together, these aspects create a cohesive vehicle identity while meeting safety and efficiency standards in the transportation industry.</p></p>
