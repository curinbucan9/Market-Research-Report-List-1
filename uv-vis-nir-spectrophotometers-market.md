<p><strong>The UV-VIS-NIR Spectrophotometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Monochromator</li><li>Double Monochromator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV-VIS-NIR spectrophotometer market primarily comprises two types: single monochromator and double monochromator systems. Single monochromator spectrophotometers utilize one monochromator to isolate wavelengths, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness for routine measurements. They are ideal for applications requiring moderate resolution. In contrast, double monochromator systems employ two monochromators to enhance wavelength resolution and reduce stray light, making them suitable for more demanding analytical applications. Both types serve diverse sectors, including pharmaceuticals, environmental monitoring, and materials science.</p></p>
