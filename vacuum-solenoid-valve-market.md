<p><p>The vacuum solenoid valve market features various types, primarily including universal direct acting and semi-direct acting valves. Universal direct acting valves operate independently, directly controlling the flow with minimal resistance, making them suitable for diverse applications. Semi-direct acting valves combine features of direct and indirect types, utilizing diaphragm action for improved flow control, offering flexibility in system designs. These valve types cater to different industrial sectors, including automotive and manufacturing, enhancing efficiency in various vacuum applications.</p></p>
