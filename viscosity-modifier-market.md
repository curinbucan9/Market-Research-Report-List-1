<p><p>The viscosity modifier market comprises several key types: </p><p>1. **Minerals** refer to natural substances like clay and calcium carbonate that enhance the flow characteristics of liquids. </p><p>2. **Hydrocolloids** are water-soluble polymers, such as xanthan gum and guar gum, that provide thickening and stabilizing properties. </p><p>3. **Synthetic Polymers** are artificially produced compounds, like polyethylene oxide, used for precise viscosity control in various applications. </p><p>4. **Others** encompass specialized modifiers, including biopolymers and additives tailored for specific industrial needs, expanding functionality across diverse sectors.</p></p>
