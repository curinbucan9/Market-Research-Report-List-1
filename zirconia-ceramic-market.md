<p><ul><li>Electro Fused Zirconium Oxide</li><li>Chemical Zirconium Oxide</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The zirconia ceramic market includes two primary types: electro-fused zirconium oxide and chemical zirconium oxide. Electro-fused zirconium oxide is produced by melting zirconia in an electric arc furnace, resulting in a dense, durable material often used in high-temperature applications and various industrial components. In contrast, chemical zirconium oxide is derived from chemical processes that yield purer zirconia, making it suitable for advanced applications like dental ceramics and electronic components, where high precision and biocompatibility are essential.</p></p>
